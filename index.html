About Us

Sanjeevani Multispeciality Hospital in Neral is a trusted name in healthcare, known for its dedication to delivering compassionate, affordable, and quality medical services. Located in a prime area, the hospital is equipped with advanced diagnostic tools, modern infrastructure, and experienced doctors across various specialties. We aim to provide patient-centered care with a focus on safety, hygiene, and holistic wellness. Whether it’s emergency care, general medicine, surgery, or maternity services, Sanjeevani ensures every patient receives personal attention and timely treatment. Our mission is to improve lives through excellence in healthcare and innovation.
